# Motion Generation

## Overview

A research record for human motion synthesis with diffusion models, semantic conditions, and animation-oriented evaluation.

## Research Questions

- Which motion representations preserve structure while remaining learnable?
- How can semantic controls remain aligned with long temporal sequences?
- Which metrics correlate with visual credibility and animation usefulness?

## Experiments

This page will track dataset processing, representation choices, conditioning methods, model variants, and visualization results.

## Current Status

The current phase focuses on mapping the research space and establishing a reliable motion visualization pipeline.
